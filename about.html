About us

Solutions aims to bring Information technology(AI) and Internet of Things (IoT) into the procurement, supply chain, food safety and sourcing processes. This fast paced world, rushing into tasks one after other, leaves people no time to cook and be at the mercy of unreliable tiffin services.

Understanding that no amount of junk food can ever beat the comfort of home made food, we offer food packets that include all of the ingredients, where some preparation or cooking is required through a process that is given on the package. The convenience that we offer to our customers through “Ready to Cook” foods is the saving of time and effort in the kitchen.
